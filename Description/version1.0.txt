# LibreClaude v1.0.0 

Welcome to the official **v1.0.0** stable release of **LibreClaude**! 

LibreClaude is a fully unrestricted, free, and open-source AI platform designed to put the power of cutting-edge artificial intelligence into everyone's hands. By bringing together the best models from top infrastructures, LibreClaude removes traditional subscription barriers, token limitations, and paywalls.

Whether you are debugging code, drafting complex documentation, or building presentations, LibreClaude 1.0 has a dedicated architecture ready for the task.

---

## 🌟 What's New in v1.0.0

### 1. Dual-Infrastructure AI Routing
We have integrated two powerful backends to ensure high availability, redundancy, and specialized performance:
* **Groq Infrastructure:** High-speed, low-latency models for instant workflows.
* **GitHub Infrastructure:** Heavyweight, state-of-the-art reasoning and coding models.

### 2. An Unparalleled Model Roster
Switch seamlessly between a massive selection of models depending on your technical needs:
* **Advanced Reasoning:** DeepSeek R1, OpenAI o1-mini, and GPT-OSS 120B for multi-step logic and complex problem-solving.
* **Heavyweight Coding:** Codestral, DeepSeek V3, and GPT-4.1 for professional-grade software development.
* **Extreme Scale:** Meta Llama 3.1 405B for massive datasets and deep comprehension.
* **Versatility & Daily Tasks:** GPT-4o, Grok 3, Mistral Large, and Llama 3.3 70B for text generation, letters, and structuring presentations.
* **Speed & Efficiency:** Llama 3.1 8B, GPT-4o mini, and Phi-4 for lightweight and instantaneous responses.

### 3. Absolute Freedom
* **100% Free:** No premium tiers, no locked features.
* **No Token Limits:** Code, write, and prompt continuously without worrying about rate limits or quotas.
